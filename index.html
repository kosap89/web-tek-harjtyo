Welcome to TORO BARBELL

TORO BARBELL is a premier powerlifting gym dedicated to helping individuals reach their strength potential. Our facilities are equipped with top-of-the-line powerlifting equipment, and our experienced coaches provide personalized training and programming to meet your individual goals.
